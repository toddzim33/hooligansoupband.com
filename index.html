About

Hooligan Soup is a four-piece rock band from Kelowna, BC, playing the songs that defined a generation — 80s through early 2000s rock and pop rock. Big guitars, big hooks, the kind of stuff that sounds like a Friday night should.

We play for the love of it. Come out, have a drink, sing something you haven't thought about in twenty years.
