What We Do

Our Services

End-to-end operational support across every dimension of your restaurant business.

Operations Audit & Assessment

A comprehensive on-site review of front of house, back of house, systems, staffing, and guest experience. Delivered with a clear, prioritized action plan.

Standard Operating Procedures

Development and documentation of clear, scalable SOPs tailored to your concept, ensuring consistency across shifts, locations, and staff changes.

Training & Staff Development

Custom training programs for line staff, supervisors, and management. From new hire onboarding to advanced leadership development and culture building.

Financial Performance & Cost Control

Deep-dive analysis of food cost, labour cost, waste, and P&L with actionable strategies to tighten margins without compromising quality or guest experience.

Menu Engineering

Menu design, item costing, pricing strategy, and product mix analysis structured to maximize profitability while delivering a compelling, cohesive guest experience.

Franchise Support & Brand Compliance

Franchisee onboarding, brand standards auditing, and multi-unit consistency programs for both franchisors building systems and franchisees navigating them.

Pre-Opening Consulting

Full launch readiness: concept refinement, hiring frameworks, training programs, systems setup, and hands-on soft-open management to start strong from day one.

Performance Recovery & Turnaround

Rapid diagnosis and structured recovery for underperforming locations, addressing operations, team culture, financials, and guest satisfaction in a focused engagement.

Leadership Coaching

One-on-one and group coaching for General Managers, Kitchen Managers, and department leads, building the people your operation depends on to perform when it matters.

Guest Experience & Service Standards

Development and implementation of hospitality standards, service culture programs, and guest satisfaction strategies that elevate every touchpoint of the dining experience.

About

Matthew Coates

Managing Director, R.O.S.S. / A Division of MPC Management

Matthew Coates brings nearly 40 years of hands-on restaurant industry experience to every client engagement. His career spans virtually every role in the business, from the floor and the line to ownership, management, and executive leadership.

Matthew has operated, managed, trained, and consulted across a remarkable breadth of environments: high-volume independents, major national and international franchise systems, high-end fine dining, and large-scale multi-unit operations. He has built training programs from scratch, launched new concepts, turned around underperforming locations, and coached hundreds of restaurant professionals into more effective leaders.

There are very few challenges in this business Matthew hasn't faced firsthand. Very few he hasn't solved.
